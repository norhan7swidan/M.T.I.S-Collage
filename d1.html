Management Technology and Information Systems - MTIS

Information Technology Systems Management - ITM

Admission: Students are accepted from the scientific department in its two divisions (science and mathematics) in high school.
Division: Students are divided into this section from the first level.
This section focuses on studying how to manage and organize information technology within organizations.
Students learn how to design and manage information technology systems that support business operations and bring efficiency and effectiveness to business management.
This department prepares students to become professionals in information technology management and includes the study of networks, information security, and database management.
