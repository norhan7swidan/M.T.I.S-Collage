Management Technology and Information Systems - MTIS

Management & Business Technology - MBT

Admission: It accepts students from the scientific and literary departments, in addition to students from commercial education.
Division: Students are divided into this section from the third level.
This section focuses on studying how to use technology to improve and develop administrative and business processes. The curriculum covers topics in strategic management, e-marketing, innovation management, and project leadership.
The department aims to prepare business leaders who can use technology to improve organizational performance and achieve success in the contemporary work environment.
