Management Technology and Information Systems - MTIS

Bussiness Information Systems - BIS

Admission:Students from the scientific and literary departments are accepted, in addition to students from commercial education.
Division: Students are divided into this section from the third level.
This section aims to teach students how to use information systems to support and manage business operations.
The curriculum focuses on developing and understanding systems that support management decisions and improve business processes. Students learn how to integrate information technologies with business strategies to improve organizational performance.
