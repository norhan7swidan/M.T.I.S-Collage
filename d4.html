Management Technology and Information Systems - MTIS

Accounting Technology - ACT

Admission: Students from the scientific and literary departments are accepted, in addition to students from commercial education.
Division: Students are divided into this section from the third level.
This department is specialized in teaching students modern accounting techniques and how to use them in the business environment.
The curriculum includes studies in financial accounting, management accounting, accounting information systems, and financial auditing.
The goal is to prepare accountants capable of using modern technology in accounting processes, preparing financial reports, and managing funds effectively.
